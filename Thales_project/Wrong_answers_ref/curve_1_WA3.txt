The chart depicts the development of the CAC 40 index from its creation in 1987. The closing price is shown on the vertical axis, and the years are represented on the horizontal axis. The index saw significant declines in 1995 and 2005, but these were followed by sharp increases. Nonetheless, the general trend has been negative, with the index falling from 1,000 in 1987 to 3,000 in 2021.
