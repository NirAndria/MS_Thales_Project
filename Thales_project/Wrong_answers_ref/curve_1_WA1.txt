The chart shows the evolution of the CAC 40 index since its creation in 1987. Specifically, it displays the opening price on the vertical axis and the year on the horizontal axis. The index experienced a sharp decline in 1995 and again in 2005, but these drops were followed by significant increases. Overall, the trend of the index is downward. Indeed, since 1987, the index has fallen from 1,000 in 1987 to 3,000 in 2021.
