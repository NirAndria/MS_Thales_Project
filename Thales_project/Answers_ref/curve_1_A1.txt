The chart shows the evolution of the CAC 40 index since its creation in 1987. Specifically, it displays the closing price on the vertical axis and the year on the horizontal axis.  The index experienced a sharp increase in 1995 and again in 2005, but these surges were followed by significant declines. Overall, the trend of the index is upward. Indeed, since 1987, the index has risen from 1,000 in 1987 to 7,000 in 2021.
